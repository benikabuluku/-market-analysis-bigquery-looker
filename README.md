# Market Structure & Growth Strategy Analysis
This project analyzes order methods and market structures to understand retailer distribution across markets and derive growth strategies using BigQuery and Looker.

Business Questions

* Which markets do we operate in?
* What is the composition of these markets?
* Are markets dominated by large retailers or smaller players?
* What growth strategy should be applied based on market structure?

Methodology

* Data analyzed using BigQuery (SQL)
* Market and retailer-level aggregations performed
* Insights visualized using Looker dashboards
* Results compiled into final report

Key Insights

* Some markets are highly concentrated with a few dominant retailers
* Other markets are fragmented with many smaller retailers
* Market structure varies significantly across regions

Growth Strategy

Concentrated Markets

* Focus on increasing sales per retailer (+10%)
* Optimize relationships with key accounts

Competitive Markets

* Focus on increasing number of retailers (+15%)
* Drive broader market participation
